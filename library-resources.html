Online Resources

List of E-Databases

  1. Manupatra: Manupatra are publishers and providers of legal, tax, corporate and business policy databases in India. Manupatra products are used in a wide variety of end markets with users ranging across Academic, Corporate, Government, Professionals (Lawyers, CA, CS etc), Banks, Consulting Companies, Media Houses, Litigants , Research Organisations, NGO’s and others.
    To access click here
  2. Westlaw: Westlaw is an online legal research service for lawyers and legal professionals. In addition, it provide proprietary database services. Information resources on Westlaw include more than 40,000 databases of case law, state and federal statutes, administrative codes, newspaper and magazine articles, public records, law journals, law reviews, treatises, legal forms and other information resources.
    To access click here
  3. Jstor: Jstor is a digital library founded in 1995. Originally containing digitized back issues of academic journals, it now also includes books and primary sources, and current issues of journals. It provides full-text searches of almost 2,000 journals.
    To access click here
