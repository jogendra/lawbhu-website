Dr. M. M. Singh

About Me

Thirteen research scholars have been awarded Ph.D. degree under my supervision during 1983-2012 and three research scholars are at present enrolled under me.The main field of my research is Corrosion and its Prevention along with electrochemistry of semiconductor(own Ph.D.),amperometry, electrodeposition and solution chemistry.In the field of corrosion, the investigated systems include; Aluminum-Nitric acid, Stainless steel-phosphoric-hydrochloric acid, SS/MS-formic-acetic acid,MS-sulphuric/ hydrochloric acid, Copper-sea water and Brass-Mine water.Cathodic protection and use of inhibitors are the two preventive methods which have been mostly employed.
